## Cart Features

### Multiple Carts
- Main Cart for immediate purchases
- Hold Bin for tracks you want to save for later

### Batch Operations
- Select multiple tracks at once
- Manage variants efficiently
- Quick checkout process

<Tip>
Use the Hold Bin to organize tracks you're interested in but not ready to purchase.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

- Regularly review your cart contents
- Use batch operations for efficiency
- Monitor your credit balance
- Check for owned tracks before purchase
