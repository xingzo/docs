## Before You Delete

Important considerations before deleting your Afro Extended account.

### Service Access
You'll lose access to:
- Active subscription benefits
- Remaining credits
- Download history
- Connected accounts

## Deletion Process

### Steps to Delete
1. Visit Security Settings
2. Choose "Delete Account"
3. Review implications
4. Confirm deletion

### Data Removal
We permanently remove:
- Personal information
- Account preferences
- Subscription details
- Download records

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All content and credits will be lost immediately.
</Warning>
