## Managing Your Crate

### Storage Limits
- 50 variants maximum
- 300MB total storage capacity
- Automatic size management

### Download Tracking
- 3 downloads per variant
- Download history integration
- Automatic cleanup of expired downloads

## Best Practices

- Monitor your storage usage
- Track remaining downloads
- Use batch operations when possible
- Review download history regularly

<Tip>
Keep an eye on your download count - each variant can be downloaded up to 3 times.
</Tip>
